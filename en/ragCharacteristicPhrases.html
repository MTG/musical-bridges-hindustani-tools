Rāg visualizer

with a focus on characteristic melodic phrases

Besides a particular set of svaras and the emphasis given to some of them, the personality of a rāg is also determined by the melodic movement between its svaras. Some of these movemes are frequently used, and become defining traits of the rāg. This tool helps you to visualize this movement across svaras, and to pay attention to the characteristic melodic phrases of the performed rāg. Read the instructions below.

Instructions

Note: This tool still is work in progress and might experience some malfunctions. For an optimal performance, it is advised to use it in a computer (its use in tablets and cellphones might be too slow and cause synchronization problems) and with the browser Chrome.
